Exclusive Groom Jewellery Collection

A wedding is a special day every person dreams of and tries to make it more special and memorable.  Not only bride is conscious of the outfit and jewellery but also groom is equally excited for the same.  In Indian wedding mostly groom is seen in that royal king look. 

In earlier time, Maharaja’s used to adorn the accessories and jewellery but now in modern times, we could not find men wearing accessories apart from the wedding day.  A wedding is the only day men wish to get that royal king look by wearing the ethnic outfits and accessories.

Let’s have a look at the accessories groom can adorn on his wedding:

Groom Necklace:

Groom necklace is also known by different names like Dulha mala, groom mala, moti mala or Sherwani mala. It is the must-have ornament for groom if he wanted to get that royal king look.  Groom necklace is available in a wonderful range of designs and pattern. Groom mala is usually made out of pearls which lends dashing look to groom.

Groom Brooch: 

Brooch is the ornament which defines the persona and it is the only ornament which is close to heart.  Groom brooch comes in a vibrant range of colour and designs. Anuradha Art Jewellery offers the wonderful collection of brooch at an affordable cost.

Turban Brooch:  

In an Indian wedding, turban plays a significant role and it is a must for a groom.  For adding more attraction to turban an ornament is attached on the front side which is known as turban brooch.  It is also known as Sarpech. Turban brooch with an upward rising design is known as Ek kalangi and that with three projections is known as teen kalangi.

Kurta Button & Cufflinks:

The accessory groom can adorn to his outfit apart from the brooch is Kurta button or cufflinks. Beautiful stone embedded cufflinks or Kurta button is available for a groom in classic designs. 

Anuradha Art Jewellery offers a wonderful collection of designer groom jewellery and accessories at the lowest price.  We customize the groom jewellery and accessories on demand.  Anuradha Art Jewellery is the only store which provides a wonderful collection of groom mala at an affordable cost.
